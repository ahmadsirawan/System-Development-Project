Who We Are?

XYZ is a sporting goods retail store that sells new and used products and provides equipment maintenance as well as other services. XYZ is your number one source for Hockey, Ski, Snowboarding, Bicycling, Racket sports, and sports Apparel. We're dedicated to providing you with the very best of sports products, with an emphasis on Value, Price, and Service.

Founded in Quebec in 1999, XYZ has come a long way from its beginnings in Mont-Tremblant. When we first started out, our passion for high-quality sports equipment drove us to start our own business.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to pass by one of our stores or contact us via email at customerservice@xyz.com
